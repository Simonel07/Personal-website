More About Me.

I am an enthusiastic young man who likes to take advantage of all the opportunities that arise. I like to devote most of my time to my biggest passions: computer science, space field and education. I try as much as possible to contribute daily with new skills to each of these passions of mine.

Speaking of computer science, I would like to tell you more about this and what I am doing in this field. I am passionate about DevOps and I would like to work and develop myself by working as a DevOps engineer, but maybe after I reach the senior level, I want to take the chance of working more in the cyber security field. Also, I think that blockchain is a rising field, and as much as nowadays this is a powerful weapon, I think that it's not already a good time to implement it everywhere, but it's good for us teenagers to learn more about it.

And now more about space and education. As you get to know me, you will realize that ROSPIN left an important mark on my life. There, I met a lot of people who helped me a lot in developing myself and gave me the opportunity to become a better version of myself. So I have continued working with them to make Romania Spatial and I have developed a personal idea for a startup.
